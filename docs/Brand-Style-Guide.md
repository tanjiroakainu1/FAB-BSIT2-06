# Food Ordering Hermanas — Brand Style Guide (UI/UX Foundation)

A cohesive look makes the app feel professional and trustworthy—crucial for a food business.

---

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Psychology |
|------|--------|------|------------|
| **Primary (Brand)** | Warm Red | `#D32F2F` | Stimulates appetite and creates urgency. |
| **Secondary** | Amber / Yellow | `#FFC107` | Friendly, warm, and associated with "Hermanas" (Sisters). |
| **Neutral** | Light Grey | `#F5F5F5` | Used for backgrounds to make food photos "pop." |
| **Success** | Green | `#388E3C` | Used for "Order Completed" and "Payment Verified." |

### Usage

- **Primary:** Main CTAs (e.g. Add to cart, Place order, Log in), headers, links, price accents.
- **Secondary:** Accent highlights, badges, or "Hermanas" branding where a warmer, sisterly feel is desired.
- **Neutral:** Page and card backgrounds so content and food images stand out.
- **Success:** Success messages (e.g. "You have successfully registered"), order completed, payment verified.

---

## Optional Extensions (for implementation)

- **Primary light:** Lighter tint for hover states (e.g. `#E53935` or derived).
- **Primary dark:** Darker shade for gradients or contrast (e.g. `#B71C1C` or derived).
- **Text on primary:** White (`#FFFFFF`) for buttons and header text on primary red.
- **Neutral borders:** Slightly darker grey (e.g. `#E0E0E0`) for borders on neutral backgrounds.
